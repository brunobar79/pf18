about me

Hey 🙂 I’m Annie, product designer from Montevideo-Uruguay. What can I tell you about me? I spent the first 4 years of my career on content, marketing and management roles in digital marketing agencies while getting my collage degree on Communications & Advertising. My work was focused on creating digital experiences from the content side, including copy and wireframes, project management, dealing with clients and working along with designers. This path made me fell in love with design and discover it was my passion. For the past 4 years, also I’ve been creating digital experiences, but crafting products from the inside as a UI/UX designer and working in different contexts; in-house product team, design studio, startups contractor. I’ve been pushing pixels, talking to users and stakeholders, working with developers, captivated by mobile apps, loving the magic of illustration, embracing design systems, mastering the art of sticking post-its, designing kickass products among other things. As to the personal ¨me¨, here are some random thoughts and qualities that define who I am. A smile always comes first when approaching people. Always be honest and speak up for yourself. Never loose your capacity for amazement. Sing if you love it, even if you suck and get voted the worst singer of the office. And last but not least… remember it’s all about connection ✨
